Quick Reference: Policy Hierarchy

POLICY
The "What" and "Why"
High-level statements of intent (Executive level)
Example: Acceptable Use Policy
STANDARD
The "Must"
Mandatory requirements or configurations
Example: Password Complexity
PRACTICE
The "How"
Step-by-step instructions (Technical level)
Example: Onboarding Procedure
